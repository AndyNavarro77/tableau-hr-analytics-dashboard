# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard — Staff Turnover | Farmacia Carvajal

> **Interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing staff turnover across roles and time periods — enabling HR teams to identify critical retention risks and reduce hiring costs through data-driven decisions.**

![Tableau](https://img.shields.io/badge/Tableau-Dashboard-E97627?logo=tableau)
![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/Status-Complete-brightgreen)

---

## 🧠 The Business Problem

High staff turnover generates direct costs — recruiting, onboarding, lost productivity — and indirect costs through institutional knowledge loss. Without clear visibility into which roles are most affected and when turnover peaks occur, HR decisions are reactive rather than preventive.

---

## ✅ The Solution

An interactive Tableau dashboard that gives HR teams real-time visibility into turnover dynamics — identifying the 5 highest-risk roles, tracking exit trends over time, and enabling proactive retention strategies before talent walks out the door.

---

## 📊 Key Metrics Monitored

- **Staff exits** — volume and monthly trend
- **Active employees** — current headcount by role
- **Exit rate** — % turnover by role and period
- **Top 5 roles with highest turnover** — priority retention targets
- **Turnover patterns over time** — seasonal and structural trends

---

## 🎯 Business Impact

| Action | Outcome |
|---|---|
| Identify top 5 high-turnover roles | Focus retention investment where it matters most |
| Track monthly exit rate trends | Anticipate peaks and plan hiring proactively |
| Segment by role and time period | Design differentiated retention strategies by area |
